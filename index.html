Our Services

Services we provide

General nursing care

The nursing team provides the following services:

  • Wound care
  • Contraceptive services
  • Blood pressure monitoring
  • INR monitoring
  • Blood tests
  • Smear tests
  • Vaccinations
Chronic disease management

Our GPs and practice nurses provide chronic illness management clinics throughout the week, which include:

  • Asthma and COPD management advice
  • Anticoagulant therapy
  • Hypertension management advice
  • Coronary heart disease management
  • Diabetes
Talking therapies

If required, your GP can recommend you to a therapist, or you can self-refer. Some talking treatments, such as depression counselling and cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), are available through the NHS. You do not need a GP referral to use an NHS psychological therapy service.

Family planning

Our physicians and nurses offer contraceptive guidance as needed. We provide coil and Nexplanon installation services. Your doctor may go through this with you in further detail. Please contact reception to schedule a consultation with a doctor.

Child development and immunisation

All of the clinicians in the practice offer child health services and developmental screenings. These are often performed at routine surgical appointments. The health visitors will do certain checks.

Maternity care / antenatal

On behalf of your doctor, our community-led midwife team manages your care. However, between six and eight weeks after your baby is born, you will require a postnatal checkup.

Smear clinics

The health authorities will automatically call you for screening if you are female and between the ages of 25 and 64. Women under 50 are summoned every three years, while those above 50 are called every five years.

Minor surgery

Minor procedures including joint injections can be performed in our specialized treatment room. Please discuss this with your primary care physician, who will schedule an appointment for you at the office. This must be done through your primary care physician.

Blood tests and results

You can schedule an appointment with one of our health care assistants, who are available at the surgery every day. Appointments for blood testing can be scheduled up to two weeks in advance. Make an appointment with the practice.

Online Services

Go to Online Consult

We've recently launched a new system called Online Consult that you can use to check your symptoms and submit forms to us. It's a new and more efficient way to contact us here at your practice, seek medical advice, request consultations with our GPs, and request administrative tasks like medical notes and reports.

  • When you access the service, you will see an online form where you may describe your symptoms. You will then have access to personalized and scientifically authorized materials and videos to help you manage your disease on your own.
  • The forms are emailed directly and securely to us at the practice, where we will review your responses and determine whether you require a face-to-face consultation with the GP, and if not, we will advise you on what to do next.
  • The forms are customized to you and have all been reviewed by physicians, ensuring that the information you receive is accurate and useful.
  • It's quite simple to use; simply click the link from our website, and the forms are plain and simple to grasp.
  • Please be aware that the Online Consult system is only available from Monday morning when the practice opens until Friday evening when the practice closes, and is not available on weekends.

Our Doctors

General practitioners (GPs) handle all common medical disorders and send patients to hospitals and other medical facilities for urgent and specialised care. They are concerned with the overall health of the individual, integrating physical, psychological, and social elements of treatment. Our GPs are highly qualified professionals who deal with medical situations where you may feel severely unwell or for follow-up on an ongoing condition. More information on our GPs, their qualifications and training, and areas of medical interest may be found below.
